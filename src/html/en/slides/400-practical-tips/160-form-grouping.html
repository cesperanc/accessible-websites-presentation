Grouping in forms

  • The form fields can be logically grouped to simplify filling. <form> <fieldset> <legend>Personal data: </legend> <p><label>Name: <input type="text" size="30"></label></p> <p><label>E-mail: <input type="text" size="30"></label></p> <p><label>Age: <input type="text" size="3"></label></p> </fieldset> </form>
  • Usually screen readers announce the legend before the label for each field
